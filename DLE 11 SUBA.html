Formal Time

Aim: to learn how to use formal time in formal situations, in which an important people asks you for the time, such as teacher, doctor, engineer, architect, president, manager, etc,.

Note: Use minutes first than hours - use past from 1 to 30. use to from 31 to 60

15 = a quarter

30 = half

Possible questions

Can you tell me the time, please?

What time is it?

Example: 7:11

It is eleven past seven

Example:1:50

It is ten to two

Example: 6:15

It is a quarter past six

Example: 8:30

It is half past eight

Example: 11:45

It is a quarter to twelve

Example: 5:00

It is five o´clock
